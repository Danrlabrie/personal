Description:

Data visualization for Exoplanets loosely based on Mike Bostock's D3 graphic. which in turn owes an homage to some other sources. Data taken from NASA archive of confirmed exoplanets. Planets are placed based on distance from earth in parsecs, and sized in earth radii per pixel. For self-evident reasons, only planets with confirmed sizes / distances are shown. Colors are based on the following scale and represent the equilibrium temperature of each planet: with white planets representing bodies with no confirmed equilibrium temperature data.

You can hover over each planet for its data. Earth is the one with the blue ping.
Zoom, drag & enjoy!
